question: What is e-invoice fiscalization?
answer: E-invoice fiscalization is a modern method of ensuring accurate and accountable financial transactions. It involves the electronic issuance and reporting of invoices to the tax authorities. With e-invoice fiscalization, businesses generate digital invoices with specific fiscal codes and transmit them directly to the tax authorities. This streamlines the invoicing process, reduces paperwork, and enhances transparency. By adopting e-invoice fiscalization, businesses contribute to a more efficient and digitally advanced economy.
